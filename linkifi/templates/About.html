Our Founders'
Story

Linkifi is a tool to help you share everything you are, in one simple link – making your online content more discoverable, easier to manage and more likely to convert. Here’s where it all began.

In 2023, brothers Alex and Anthony Zaccaria, along with their business partner Nick Humphreys, identified a common challenge while managing social media accounts for clients at their digital agency, Bolster. Constantly updating individual bio links was not only time-consuming but often overlooked. From this frustration, the first version of Linkifi was born, and the rest is history!

Today, with over 30 million users globally, we’re empowering brands, artists, publishers, agencies, and influencers to take full control of their online presence. As the internet landscape became more fragmented, Linkifi evolved to offer more streamlined solutions for content sharing.

Our passionate team of developers, designers, marketers, and business professionals is dedicated to providing creators with the best tools to showcase their work. Headquartered in Melbourne, Australia, we also have offices in Sydney and Los Angeles.

In 2023, we were honored to be recognized in CNBC’s ‘Upstart 100’ list, highlighting the most promising young startups. In March 2024, we were thrilled to be named by Fast Company in their ‘Most Innovative Companies of 2024’ list, where we ranked fourth in the Social Media category. Previous honorees in this category include industry leaders like Glossier and Reddit.
